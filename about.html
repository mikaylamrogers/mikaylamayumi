About





Mikayla Mayumi is a Filipino-American creative technologist with roots in Philadelphia, Bangkok, Honolulu, and Manila, specializing in interaction design and front-end web development.

She is currently studying Integrated Digital Media (IDM) with minors in Technology, Management, & Design (TMD) and Web Programming & Applications at the NYU Tandon School of Engineering.

She recently spent a year on exchange, studying at the Department of Computer Science at the City University of Hong Kong, and at the Faculty of Humanities at the University of Amsterdam.
